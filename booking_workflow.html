📊 Complete Process Flow
1. Customer Journey:
Customer accesses website → Creates booking session → Selects seats → Completes booking entry
2. Insurance & Payment:
Fills insurance form → Completes payment notification
3. Admin Review (Decision Point):
Backoffice admin reviews and approves/rejects booking
4. Final Steps:
✓ If Approved → Final booking confirmation → Send email/SMS notification to customer
✗ If Rejected → Process refund and notify customer
🔵 Blue Boxes
Customer-facing steps (Website, Session, Seat Selection)
🟠 Orange Boxes
Booking details & payment processing
🔴 Pink Box
Admin review & approval decision point
🟢 Green Boxes
Successful booking confirmation
💡 Key Point: The Admin Approval step is the critical decision point where bookings are either confirmed or rejected in the backoffice system.
